# Disclaimer

This MCP server provides veterinary medicines data for informational purposes only.

## Not Professional Advice

The data served by this tool does not constitute professional veterinary, pharmaceutical, or food safety advice. Withdrawal periods, dosing information, and regulatory data are based on published VMD (Veterinary Medicines Directorate) and UK government sources, but individual circumstances vary. **Always check the specific Summary of Product Characteristics (SPC) for your product and dose.** Always consult a qualified veterinary surgeon before making prescribing decisions.

## Food Safety Warning

Wrong withdrawal periods can lead to medicine residues in meat, milk, eggs, and honey entering the human food chain. The data in this server is a reference guide -- it does not replace the product SPC or veterinary advice. If in doubt, extend the withdrawal period and seek professional guidance.

## Currency

Veterinary medicine authorisations change. Products may be withdrawn, suspended, or have their SPCs amended. This tool shows the data available at the time of its last ingestion run. Check `check_data_freshness` for the age of the data.
